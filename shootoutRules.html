Summary of Sweepstakes Rules

  • No purchase necessary. Eligibility and other restrictions apply. See official “Sweepstakes Rules” for more information.

  • The sweepstakes begins March 7, 2025, and ends April 20, 2025.
  • The seven finalists will be drawn shortly after the sweepstakes ends.
  • Finalists will be selected as follows:
    • Two (2) from Arizona
    • Two (2) from Michigan
    • One (1) from Illinois
    • One (1) from Missouri
    • One (1) from Nevada
  • Travel and accommodations will be provided to the winners and a guest.
  • The winners will have the opportunity to participate in the “Hole-In-One Shootout” on Saturday, May 24, 2025, in Mesa, Arizona.
  • A hole-in-one wins $1,000,000!
